#### Movement
$W = \Delta K = \frac{1}{2}m ( v_{f} - v_{i})^{2}$

$e$ is energy in Joules. Kinetic energy ($K$/$K_{e}$), electric potential energy ($U_{elec}$) etc.
- $e = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2}$
- $v = \sqrt{ \frac{2e}{m} }$
- $m = \frac{2e}{v^{2}}$
