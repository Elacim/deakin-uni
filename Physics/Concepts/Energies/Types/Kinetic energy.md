**Which has the larger kinetic energy?**
A 10g bullet at 500m/s OR a 10kg bowling ball at 10m/s?

Bullet: $=0.5 * (10*10^{-3}) * 500^{2} = 1250J$
Bowling ball: $=0.5 * 10 * 100 = 500J$
