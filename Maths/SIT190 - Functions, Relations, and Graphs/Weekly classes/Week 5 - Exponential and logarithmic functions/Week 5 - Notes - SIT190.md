#### Compound interest
$P$ = initial investment
$r$ = rate (%)
$m$ = number of times it's compounded
$t$ = period of time (yearly)
$A = P(1 + \frac{r}{m})^{mt}$

$2P = P(1 + \frac{1}{20})^{4t}$

#### Exponential growth
$y = Ae^{kx}$
Domain: $x \epsilon R$
Range: $y > 0$
Intercepts: $(1, 0)$ (no x intercepts)

**Inverse**
$y = log_{a}(x)$
Domain $x > 0$
Range: $y \epsilon R$
Intercepts: $(1, -)$ (no y-intercepts)
