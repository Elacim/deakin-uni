$y = x^{2} - 5x + 7$
Shape: Parabola
Discriminant: $\sqrt{-5^{2} - 4(1)(7)} = \sqrt{25 - 28} = \sqrt{-3}$
	Negative discriminants mean there's no x-intercept
x-intercept: -
y-intercept: +7
Stationary point: $x = \frac{-b}{2a}$, $\frac{--5}{2(1)} = \frac{5}{2}$
	$\frac{5}{2}^{2} - 5\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)+ 7$
	= 6.25 - (25/2) + 7 = 3/4 
	-> $(\frac{5}{2}, \frac{3}{4})$
