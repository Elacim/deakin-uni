##### $y=ln(3x)$
Find the:
- y-intercept,
- x-intercept,
- the Domain, and
- the Range

**y-intercept**
$y=ln(3x)$
-> $y=ln(3*0)$
$y=\text{undefined}$

**x-intercept**
$y=ln(3x)$
-> $0=ln(3x)$
-> $e^{0} = e^{ln(3x)}$
-> $1=3x$
$x=\frac{1}{3}$

**Domain**
Logs with real number results cannot have negative inputs, so, $x$ must be $> 0$ 

**Range**
Logs without parameters can range from $-\infty < y < \infty$
