$a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^{n}}$ given $a \neq 0$
$ba^{-n} = \frac{b}{a^{n}}$
##### Examples
- $4^{-2} = \frac{1}{4^{2}}$
- $7x^{-3} = \frac{7}{x^{3}}$
- $-7x^{-3} = \frac{-7}{x^{3}} = -\frac{7}{x^{3}}$
- $-12\sqrt{3}e^{2(-\sqrt{3})} = -12\sqrt{3}e^{-2\sqrt{3}} = - \frac{12\sqrt{3}}{e^{2\sqrt{3}}}$
