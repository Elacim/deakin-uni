##### Exponential growth/decay
$y = Ae^{kx}$
Domain: $x \epsilon R$

##### Inverse
$y = log_{a}(x)$
Domain $x > 0$
Range: $y \epsilon R$
Intercepts: $(1, -)$ (no y-intercepts)
