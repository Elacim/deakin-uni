#### Sexagesimal
$,$ = separator
$;$ = decimal point

$1,0 = 60L$

$1; 24, 51, 10$
$= (1 \times 60^ {0})+ (24 \times 60^{-1}) + (51 \times 60^{-2}) + (10 \times 60^{-3})$
$= 1 + 0.4 + 0.0141 + 0.0000463 = 1.414146$
