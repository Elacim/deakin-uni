**Products of functions**
i.e. One function multiplied by another
$x^{5} \times \sin(x)$

**Quotients of functions**
i.e. One function divided over another
$\frac{\sin(4)}{x^{2}}$

**Functions of functions (chains)**
i.e. One function applied to another

$\sin(2x - 1)$
$e^{4x+3}$
$\ln(2x^{2} + 7x - 1)$
